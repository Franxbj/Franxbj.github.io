About Us

Aurora Art Gallery showcases contemporary Finnish art in the heart of Mikkeli. Our carefully curated collection features emerging and established artists working across traditional and digital media.

Founded in 2024, we believe art should be accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of curation. Our intimate space provides the perfect environment for contemplation and discovery.
