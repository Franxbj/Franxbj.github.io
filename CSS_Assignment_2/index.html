Artist Spotlight

Elena Virtanen

Elena Virtanen, a contemporary abstract artist from Helsinki, channels the pulse of nature into her canvases. Her work is a dialogue between landscapes and emotion, where forests, skies, and rivers dissolve into color. Bold hues surge across the surface like living currents, while dynamic forms fracture and reassemble into new visions. Each painting becomes a threshold — inviting viewers to step into a realm where memory, place, and feeling converge.

“I paint not what I see, but what I feel — landscapes are only the beginning.” — Elena Virtanen
