How to Code a Sandcastle

Pearl wants to build the perfect sandcastle at the beach, but she needs help from her robot friend Pascal. Together, they learn how to break down the big task into smaller steps using coding concepts.

When things don't go as planned, Pearl discovers the importance of debugging and trying again. She learns that making mistakes is part of the process and that perseverance is key to success.The story makes coding fun and accessible by connecting it to a familiar activity that all children understand. Enjoy the Read Aloud Video.

    List of Characters

  • Pearl (the main character, a creative and determined girl)
  • Pascal (Pearl's robot friend, who helps her with coding concepts)
  • Pearl's Mom (appears in the background at the beach)

    List of Memorable Scenes

  1. Pearl arrives at the beach excited to build the best sandcastle ever
  2. Pearl meets Pascal the robot and asks him to help build her sandcastle
  3. Pearl learns to give Pascal step-by-step instructions (sequences) to gather sand and water
  4. Pascal makes mistakes and Pearl discovers she needs to "debug" her instructions
  5. Pearl uses loops to make Pascal repeat actions efficiently
  6. The sandcastle is finally complete and Pearl celebrates her success
